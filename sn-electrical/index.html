03 · How it worksThe system, in motion.

The ICP shapes the Sheet. The Sheet and the Project work together, two-way. From there, three workflows you'll use again and again, each producing something you can act on.

The system in one picture

Three things matter about this. The Sheet is the memory: every output is shaped by what's already in it. The connection is two-way: the Project reads the Sheet at the start of every conversation and writes back when you tell it to. You're always steering: nothing happens autonomously, no surprises. After your Xero experience, that's the point.

04 · How we'll do itTwo weeks, four steps.

01 Scoping call 30 minutes · before we start

A short call before any work begins. We sharpen the ICP together, pick the tool (Claude or ChatGPT), and confirm Gmail and Calendar access. I'll ask you to walk me through how you'd pitch Sunbury Honda so the tone is yours, not mine. By the end of the call, I have everything I need.

02 Build on my side 2 days · I work, you don't

I draft the ICP document, run a deep research pass on Melbourne dealerships, and populate the Sheet with 150 to 250 ranked prospects. The Sheet itself gets built out: tabs, dropdowns, conditional formatting, scripts and templates on side tabs.

Then I build the Project skills. Four of them: research new prospects, sequence a drive-by, generate a call script, draft an email. Each one is tested end to end against real prospects, tuned for your voice and accent. By the time you see it, I've already run it.

03 Handover with you 2 hours · together, in person if we can

We sit down somewhere quiet. Set up the Project on your account, connect your Drive, Gmail, and Calendar. Run a live drive-by plan together. Draft a real email for a real prospect. Then we walk outside and you voice an update from the carpark, just to prove it works in the wild. You leave with it running.

04 One week check-in 30 minutes · phone or message

A week later. You've used it, you've found the awkward bits. We talk through what's working, what's broken, what's missing. I tune what needs tuning. Anything bigger goes into a separate conversation about a next step, not this one.

This is yours to run. After the handover and the one-week tune, you can keep this going on your own indefinitely. No subscription to me, no dependency. The Project, the Sheet, and the ICP all live in your accounts.
